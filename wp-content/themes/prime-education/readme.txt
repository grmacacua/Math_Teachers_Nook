== Description ==

Prime Education is a versatile and comprehensive WordPress theme tailored for educational institutions, tutors, and online learning platforms. Designed to cater to the diverse needs of the education sector, this theme offers a user-friendly interface and robust features to create stunning and functional websites effortlessly. With its intuitive design and flexible layout options, the Prime Education WordPress Theme is suitable for various educational purposes, including schools, colleges, universities, e-learning platforms, and individual educators. Its responsive design ensures optimal viewing experiences across all devices, making it accessible to students, parents, and educators alike. Visually, the Prime Education WordPress Theme boasts a modern and professional aesthetic, with clean lines, crisp typography, and vibrant color schemes. The layout is thoughtfully organized to prioritize important information such as courses, schedules, faculty profiles, and contact details, facilitating easy navigation for visitors. One of the key benefits of the Prime Education WordPress Theme is its customizable nature, allowing users to tailor the website to match their branding and preferences seamlessly. From customizing colors and fonts to choosing layout options and page templates, users have the flexibility to create a unique and visually appealing online presence. Moreover, the theme offers integration with popular plugins and tools, such as WooCommerce for selling courses or educational materials, and Learning Management System (LMS) plugins for creating and managing online courses.

== Installation ==
        
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
